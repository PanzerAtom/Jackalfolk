Jackalfolk

Jackalfolk are humanoids that strongly resemble wild jackals, with similar heads, tails, legs and fur colouring. Jackalfolk do not typically have their own society, as they have melded almost perfectly with the other humanoids that inhabit the sands they call home, though occasionally one may find a secluded sect of them living on their own. These small villages are usually centered around a great shrine of one of the gods, in most cases that god being Anubis, or whatever god of death may call the region their domain. Many Jackalfolk are highly religious, even the city-dwelling ones usually having a small shrine or token of their patron god in their homes. Jackalfolk also like to decorate their bodies with golden paint, typically around their eyes, necks, and where their limbs meet their torso.

Ability Score Increase.Your Dexterity increases by 2, and your Wisdom score is increased by 1
Age.Jackalfolk have somewhat longer lifespans, and grow slower, than humans. Jackalfolk reach physical maturity around the age of 20, but many do not die until the age of 250. Their fur begins to grey as they approach 150 years.
Alignment.Jackalfolk typically lean towards good alignments, though they have free choice.
Size.Jackalfolk stand between 6 and 7.5 feet tall and weigh roughly the same as a large human.
Speed.Your base walking speed is 30 feet.
DarkvisionYour feral counterpart is well suited for hunting at night, and as such your eyes are adapted to low light conditions. You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can't discern color in darkness, only shades of gray. Animal Senses.Your sense of smell and hearing is better than most, granting you advantage on Wisdom (Perception), checks involving these senses.
The Desert Is Your Home. Jackalfolk are a desert-living race, and as such are well-adapted to extremely hot weather conditions, droughts, and feel more at home in sandy environments. You gain advantage on Survival checks made in desert conditions.
